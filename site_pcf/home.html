Secretariat du Parti Communiste Francais

Transcription and encoding of the minutes of the meeting of the French Communist Party by Adonis Dulaurent

This project constitutes an encoding proposal for the digitized meeting minutes of the French Communist Party’s Secretariat, preserved at the Departmental Archives of Seine-Saint-Denis (France). Developed as part of the XSLT coursework within the Master’s program in Digital Technologies Applied to History (TNAH) at the École Nationale des Chartes, this initiative aims to structure and enhance historical records through standardized markup practices.The digitized minutes, accessible via the archives’ online platform , serve as the primary source material for this encoding effort, which seeks to improve both analytical capabilities and long-term preservation of these documents.
